Based on VRChat metaverse, for Teochew players everywhere

VRChat is a free global social video game on the Steam. When we Teochew players first play VRChat, we may suffer from difficulties such as language barriers, not knowing how to play, and having no playmates. Our ancestors, who left their homes to make a living overseas hundreds of years ago, faced similar challenges. Therefore, we decided to establish a Teowchew Association in the metaverse, just like our ancestors ever did, gathering Teochew players from China and overseas. Feel free to join us for fun!
